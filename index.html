About the team

Futbol Club Barcelona is a professional football club, based in Barcelona. Catalonia, Spain. Foundered in 1899 by a group of Swiss, English and Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper, the has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence the motto “Mes que un club”(more than a club). Unlike many other football clubs, the supporters own and operate Barcelona. It is the second most valuable sport team in the world, worth $3.56 billion, and the world’s second richest football club in term of revenue, with an annual turnover of €560.8 million. The official Barcelona anthem is the”Cant del Barcelona”, written by Jaume Picas and Josep Maria Espinas.

Domestically, Barcelona has won 24 La Liga, 28 Copa del Rey, 12 Supercopa de Espana, 3 Copa Eva Duarte and 2 Copa de la Liga trophies, as well as being the record holder for the latter four competitions. In international club football, Barcelona has won five UEFA Champions League titles, a record four UEFA Cup Winners’ Cup, a shared record five UEFA Super Cup, a record three inter-Cites Fairs Cup and a record three FIFA Club World Cup, a shared record two latin cup trophies. Barcelona was ranked first in the IFFHS Club World Ranking for 1997, 2009, 2011, 2012, and 2015 ans currently occupies the third position on the UEFA club rankings. The club has a long-standing rivalry with Real Madrid; matches between the two teams are referred to as El Clasico.
